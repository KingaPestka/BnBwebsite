About Karen

Karen Peters is the owner of a unique Bed & Breakfast (B&B) located on the west coast of Ireland. Karen's buisness is a family run B&B,in operation for the past 20 years that offers spectacular views over Galway Bay. Karen is a very well known and respected B&B owner in the area, She often participates in community fund raisers and helps organise annual youth festivals. Ever since she was young, karen dreamed of running a hotel ,however , over the years she realised that a B&B provides a more homely feeling and a friendly athmosphere. In the recent years Karen’s business was awarded a TripAdvisor Certificate of Excellence in 2018 and 2019.

We offer the following rooms:

  • The Wild Atlantic Way Suite €68 Per Night- Single Room – sleeps max of 1 person. Own private balcony.
  • The Moher Suite €114 Per Night - Double or Twin Room – sleeps max of 2 people.
  • The West Suite €152 Per Night- Triple Room – sleeps max of 3 people.​​Three single beds or one double bed and one single bed provided.
  • The Connacht Suite €210 Per Night - Family Room – sleeps max of 2 adults and 2 children. One double and two single beds. Cots available on request.

    • All rooms in the B&B offer luxuriously furnished, non-smoking, own private bathrooms with full size baths, separate walk-in tropical rain showers, walnut wooden floors, central heating, iron and ironing board, hairdryer, 40" smartT.V. and tea/coffee making facilities . Full breakfast is included,free 24 hour wireless broadband Internet access throughout your stay and free private off street car park. Everything including tax is already included in the rate.

    Facilities nearby the B&B include : Eyre Square Shopping Centre, two sandy beaches, spectacular coastal walks, a variety of restaurants within walking distance, friendly traditional Irish music pubs, horse-riding, golfing, fishing, and a variety of water sports . Bikes are available for hire.
