Q . Between what times is the breakfast served?

Breakfast is served between 7.00am and 10.30am weekdays and 7.30am to 11:00am at weekends (Saturdays and Sundays).

Q . Is there any cancellation fee?

All cancellation is free of charge.

Q . How are tours arranged?

Sight seeing tours can be arranged at the B&B at a discount for:Connemara, CliffsofMoher, TheAran Islands and a bus tour of Galway City.All tickets can be purchased on arrival.

Q . Do you offer any recreational activites? if so do you offer any for children?

We offer horse-riding, golfing, fishing, and a variety of water sports . Bikes are also available for hire. For children , we have a play area and and outdoor play ground, nanny's are avaiable for hire.

Q . Can we Bring our Pets?

All pets are welcome! Big and Small.However,be aware that you will have to sign a form which indicates you take full responsability for any damage caused by your pet.

Q . Is there Parking and is it free?

There is a Free Private Parking area just 2 minutes away from the B&B.
